About EcoScan

EcoScan is a smart waste-sorting web and mobile app that helps users identify recyclable, biodegradable, and non-recyclable materials simply by scanning an image. It uses AI-based classification to guide people towards proper waste disposal — promoting sustainability and eco-awareness.

Key Features

AI Waste Detection

Instantly classifies waste as recyclable, biodegradable, or non-recyclable.

Eco Tips

Provides smart disposal and recycling tips for each item scanned.

Sustainability Focused

Aligned with UN SDG 12 & 13 for responsible consumption and climate action.

Gamified Rewards

Encourages eco-friendly behavior with reward points for correct segregation.
